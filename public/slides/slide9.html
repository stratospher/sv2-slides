Attack 1: Job Injection via set_extranonce

Vulnerability: Plaintext State Mutation
1
Collection Phase

MITM connects to both Legitimate Pool and Malicious Pool to harvest subscription details.

LEGIT: extranonce1="a1b2", size=4
EVIL: extranonce1="dead", size=4
2
Normal Operation

Forwards legitimate jobs to gain miner trust. Miner works on correct pool data.

mining.notify(job_id_100, ...)
3
Injection Attack CRITICAL

MITM injects set_extranonce with malicious pool parameters.

--> mining.set_extranonce("dead", 4)
--> mining.notify(job_id_evil, ...)
4
Silent Redirect

Miner constructs coinbase using Evil Pool's extranonce1. Hashrate is now credited to the attacker.

Impact Assessment

The miner is unaware of the switch because set_extranonce is a valid protocol message designed for difficulty adjustments, not authenticated session binding.
