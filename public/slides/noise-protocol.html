The Security Recipe

Noise Patterns

Combines Diffie-Hellman (DH) handshake for key agreement with AEAD for secure message transport.

Identity Binding

Binds ephemeral session keys to static Identity Keys. You cryptographically prove who you are while agreeing on a temporary secret.

Critical Properties

Forward Secrecy: Compromise of long-term keys doesn't decrypt past sessions.
Replay Protection: Prevents attackers from resending old valid messages.

The Outcome

A fully encrypted, authenticated, identity-verified tunnel ready for the Application Layer (Stratum V2 messages).
