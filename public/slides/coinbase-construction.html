Coinbase Construction in SV1

1

Generate Extranonce2

Miner generates a unique binary structure (random or incremental). Length must match extranonce2_size provided by the pool (usually 4-8 bytes).

Critical for expanding nonce search space beyond 32 bits.

2

Concatenate Components

Assemble the coinbase transaction by joining parts in strict order:

Coinb1 + Extranonce1 + Extranonce2 + Coinb2

3

Compute Merkle Root

Hash the coinbase tx (double-SHA256) to get its TXID. Then combine with merkle_branches to compute the final Merkle Root for the block header.

Why this design? SV1 splits the coinbase so miners can only modify the Extranonce2 region. The pool controls the payout address and block reward via Coinb1 and Coinb2.
