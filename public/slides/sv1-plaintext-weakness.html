2012 Design Limitations

Historical Context

Designed to replace getwork for efficiency, not security. Encryption was an afterthought in the early ASIC era.

Total Eavesdropping

Passive observers on the network can read everything:

· Worker credentials (user / pass)
· Incoming jobs & difficulty targets
· Submitted shares (proof of work)

Tampering & Identity Risks

Attackers can modify jobs on the fly. Miners cannot verify they are talking to the real pool — no cryptographic authentication.
