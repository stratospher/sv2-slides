1. Key Generation

P1 = k1 · G

P2 = k2 · G

G is the Generator Point on the Elliptic Curve.

2. Shared Secret Calculation

S = k1 · P2

Substitute P2:

= k1 · (k2 · G)

Commutative Property:

= k2 · (k1 · G)

= k2 · P1

One-Way Function

Given P and G, it is computationally infeasible to calculate k (Discrete Logarithm Problem).

Privacy

An observer sees P1, P2, and G, but cannot compute S without k1 or k2.
