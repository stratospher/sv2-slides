Defense Mechanisms

No Decryption (Confidentiality)

Attacker lacks the Noise session keys. The Diffie-Hellman exchange ensures only the Miner and Pool share the encryption secret. Forward secrecy protects past sessions.

No Modification (Integrity)

Every frame includes an AEAD authentication tag. If an attacker modifies even a single bit (e.g., changing a job ID), the verification fails, and the connection is dropped.

No Impersonation (Authenticity)

The attacker cannot complete the handshake without the Pool's Private Authority Key. Miners verify the Pool's static public key before sending shares.
