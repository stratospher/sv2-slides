On-Path Positioning

The attacker must sit between the miner and the pool (ISP, LAN, Rogue Wi-Fi, or BGP hijacking) to intercept the TCP connection on port 3333.

Plaintext Visibility

Because Stratum V1 sends JSON in cleartext, the attacker can parse every message type (subscribe, authorize, submit) in real-time.

Dual Sessions

The attacker maintains two active upstream connections:

  • Legit Pool: To proxy normal traffic and keep miner happy.
  • Evil Pool: To receive malicious jobs for injection attacks.
