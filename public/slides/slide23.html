Built on Noise Framework

Binary Framed Messages

Replaces verbose JSON-RPC with compact binary frames. Reduces bandwidth usage by ~50% and eliminates parsing ambiguity.

Mandatory Encryption & Integrity

Every byte is encrypted via AEAD (ChaCha20-Poly1305). No plaintext commands, preventing eavesdropping and tampering.

Expanded Roles

Explicitly defines Translator (V1-to-V2) and Proxy roles for efficient aggregation and migration.

Modular Subprotocols

Separates concerns: Mining (standard work), Job Negotiation (block construction), and Template Distribution.
